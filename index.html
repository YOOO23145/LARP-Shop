🎥 Content Creator Program

To all Los Angeles content creators—whether you're big or small—this is for you!

Use our server as a recording ground for your content and film amazing, realistic roleplays with our incredible members and staff. Your content will be showcased to the server where many members will watch and stream it.

How to Apply:

  • Open a management ticket
  • State which platform your content is on
  • Provide a link to your content

Once approved, you’ll receive the @LARP | Content Creator role, and your content will be reviewed by the staff team daily.

Note: Requirements may change in future.
